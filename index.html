Family Recipes, Built From Scratch

Tres Tacos LLC started in 2019 with one truck, one griddle, and three generations of recipes brought over from Jalisco. What began as weekend pop-ups at local car shows grew into a full-time drive-thru operation once regulars started lining up before we even opened the window.

Every salsa is made in-house daily, every tortilla is pressed fresh, and every order comes through our drive-thru window fast — without cutting corners on flavor. We're still run day-to-day by the same family that started it, and we still taste-test every new item on the menu before it goes up on the board.

Good To Know

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to stay in my car?

Yes — Tres Tacos LLC is drive-thru only at our Losee Rd location. There's no walk-up window or seating on site, though we do offer catering for events off-site.

What forms of payment do you accept?

Cash, all major credit/debit cards, and tap-to-pay (Apple Pay/Google Pay) at the window.

Do you have vegetarian options?

Yes. Our grilled nopales (cactus) tacos are vegetarian, and rice, beans, chips & guac, elote, and most sides are meat-free. Let us know at the window if you have dietary restrictions.

How long is the wait?

Since everything is cooked to order, plan on about 5 minutes during regular hours. Weekend evenings can run a bit longer during peak times.

Can I place a large order ahead of time?

For orders of 10+ people, call us at (702) 555-8226 at least a day in advance so we can have it ready when you arrive.
